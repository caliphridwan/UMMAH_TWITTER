ABOUT US

Ummah Twitter

The Muslim Ummah Twitter is a non-profit association dedicated to promoting understanding and tolerance among Muslims on Twitter. We believe that Twitter can be a powerful tool for building bridges between different cultures and faiths, and we are committed to using this platform to foster positive dialogue and interaction.

Muslim Ummah Twitter was founded a couple of years back, notably early 2020, by a group of Muslim Twitter users who were concerned about the rising tide of Islamophobia and hate speech on the platform. We believe that it is more important than ever to have a strong presence on Twitter to counter these negative messages and promote a more positive image of Islam.

Muslim Ummah Twitter's mission is to:

  • Provide a safe and welcoming space for Muslims on Twitter
  • Promote understanding and tolerance among Muslims and non-Muslims
  • Counter Islamophobia and hate speech on Twitter
  • Empower Muslims to use Twitter to share beneficial stories and experiences.

    • OUR ACTIVITIES include:

  • Organizing Twitter spaces and events.
  • Producing educational materials about Islam and Muslims.

    • OUR VALUES


    Ummah Twitter is committed to the following values:

  • Understanding:
    • We believe that it is important to understand different cultures and faiths in order to build bridges between them. We strive to create a space where Muslims and non-Muslims can learn from each other and respectfully share their perspectives.
  • Tolerance:
    • We believe that everyone, regardless of their faith, deserves to be treated with respect and dignity. We condemn all forms of discrimination and hate speech.
  • Peace:
    • We believe that peace is the only way to achieve a just and equitable world. We work to promote peace and understanding particularly among Muslims and also non-Muslims, and we stand against all forms of violence and conflict.

    How Can I Support Ummah Twitter?

    • Following our scholars for more audience
    • Joining our twitter daily and weekly spaces
    • Attending our events
    • Donating to endorsed and authenticated muslim organizations
    • Well-presented advice on the betterment of our programs
    • Patronize brothers and sisters' businesses on the timeline.
